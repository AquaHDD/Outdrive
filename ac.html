AC Repairs


Effective air conditioning is an essential comfort in the Indian summer. AC problems can be caused due to minor issues as well as from major electrical failures. Many of these can be prevented with periodic AC servicing.
Minor Causes:
Low refrigerant level, chocked filters, leaks in hoses/pipes/ joints or blown fuses/relays etc.
Major Causes:
Damaged condenser, evaporator coil, compressor failure, malfunctioning refrigerant valve, bad blower motor, wiring damage etc.
